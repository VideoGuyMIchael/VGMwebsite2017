Michael Dulin

"After Effects is my playground and keyframes are my best friends."

I am a storyteller. Motion is my instrument of choice when crafting stories that bring brands to life, illustrate personalities, and delight audiences around the world.


As an expert After Effects user and beta tester for Adobe, I have taught classes in motion design, given professional talks, and crafted motion strategies for fortune 500 companies.



Designing for the marketing, retail, events, and digital product arenas has given me a panorama view of designing for every step in the customer journey.




“If you're not prepared to be wrong, you'll never come up with anything original.” Ken Robinson

Good design is a series of failures. Every product I have launched was built on the foundation of thousands of scrapped designs. I see each failure as a step towards my goal, a well-thoughout customer focused design.
